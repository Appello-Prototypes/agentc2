# DSAR Procedure (Access, Correction, Deletion)

## Intake

1. Receive request via support channel.
2. Verify requester identity and organization relationship.
3. Open tracked DSAR case with due date.

## Processing

1. Enumerate relevant data sources (account, runs, support, integrations).
2. Export data for access requests in a structured format.
3. For correction requests, update authoritative records and confirm completion.
4. For deletion requests, remove data unless legal hold or contractual constraints apply.

## SLAs

- Initial acknowledgment: 3 business days.
- Completion target: 30 calendar days.
- Extension (if required): communicated with reason before due date.

## Verification and Closure

- Two-person review for high-impact deletions.
- Capture evidence: requester verification, scope, actions taken, completion timestamp.
- Notify requester of completion and any exceptions.
