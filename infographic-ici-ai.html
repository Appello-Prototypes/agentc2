The work that gets you paid

Skilled trades and field labour remain largely immune to AI. These roles require physical presence, spatial reasoning, and hands-on problem solving that AI cannot replicate.

The Overhead Paradox

In a typical ICI project, 15–25% of total project cost is overhead — estimating, project management, accounting, procurement, and administration. These are the exact functions where 50–75% of tasks can be augmented by AI. The work AI disrupts most is the work that drives your costs up, not the work that earns revenue.

AI won't replace your crews.
It will shrink your overhead.

ICI contractors who adopt AI in the back office can reduce overhead ratios, bid more competitively, and scale operations — all without adding headcount. The question isn't whether AI will impact construction. It's whether you'll use it before your competitors do.
